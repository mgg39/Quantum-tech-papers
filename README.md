# Quantum-tech-papers
This repo contains some of the papers and publications, I have read over the past few months, that pertain to quantum technology development.
It is not a curated list of the most recommended publications for a beginner in the quantum networking field, but rather a journal (of sorts) that depics my journey through publications.
Re-curring topics on this list are:

## Quantum Networking and Internet
- [Quantum Networking by Rodney Van Meter](https://www.amazon.com/Quantum-Networking-Rodney-Van-Meter/dp/1848215373) (book)
- [The Race to Build the Quantum Internet by Jonathan Dowling](https://www.amazon.com/Schr%C3%B6dingers-Web-Race-Quantum-Internet/dp/022666515X) (book)
- [Quantum Network Protocols by Wojciech Kozlowski, et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.02575)

## Quantum Algorithms and Computing
- [A Grand Unification of Quantum Algorithms by John M. Martyn, et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.02859)
- [Quantum Computational Supremacy by Aram W. Harrow, Ashley Montanaro](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1809.07442.pdf)
- [Quantum Algorithm for Linear Systems of Equations by Aram W. Harrow, et al.](https://arxiv.org/pdf/0811.3171.pdf)

## Quantum Machine Learning and Data Processing
- [Quantum Machine Learning Classification in Finance by Javier Mancilla, Christophe Pere](https://www.mdpi.com/1099-4300/24/11/1656)
- [Quantum Support Vector Machine for Big Data Classification](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.113.130503)
- [Quantum Computational Advantage with a Programmable Photonic Processor](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-022-04725-x)

## Quantum Error Correction and Mitigation
- [Quantum Error Correction Tutorial by Andrew M. Steane](https://www2.physics.ox.ac.uk/sites/default/files/ErrorCorrectionSteane06.pdf)
- [Real-Time Quantum Error Correction Beyond Break-Even](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.09116)
- [Error Correction of Quantum Algorithms by Andrew K. Tan, et al.](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2301.08542.pdf)

## Quantum Hardware and Technology
- [Advances in Silicon Quantum Photonics by Jeremy C. Adcock, et al.](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9205209)
- [Ion Traps for Quantum Computing](https://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/9708022) (general reference)
- [Silicon-based Quantum Computing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.11753)

## Quantum Theory and Foundations
- [Constructor Theory of Information by David Deutsch, Chiara Marletto](https://arxiv.org/abs/1405.5563)
- [Quantum Mechanics with Real Numbers by Vlatko Vedral](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2308.05473.pdf)
- [Observable Thermalization in Quantum Systems by Lodovico Scarpa, et al.](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2309.15173.pdf)

## Quantum Communication and Cryptography
- [Quantum Communications and Cryptography by Alexander V. Sergienko](https://people.bu.edu/alexserg/Impression_Book.pdf)
- [Advances in Quantum Cryptography by S. Pirandola, et al.](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1906.01645.pdf)

## Quantum Networks and Repeater Architectures
- [All-photonic Quantum Repeaters by Koji Azuma, et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/1309.7207)
- [Multiplexed Quantum Repeaters by Prajit Dhara, et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.06707)

## Quantum Simulations and Experimental Tools
- [Experimental Demonstration of Memory-Enhanced Quantum Communication](https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.01323)
- [Quantum Simulator for Observing Charge Carriers Pairing](https://www.mpq.mpg.de/6803661/01-magnetic-pairing)

## Quantum Market, Hype, and Commercial Perspectives
- [Commercial Applications of Quantum Computing by Francesco Bova, et al.](https://epjquantumtechnology.springeropen.com/articles/10.1140/epjqt/s40507-021-00091-1)
- [Disentangling Hype from Practicality in Quantum Computing by Torsten Hoefler, et al.](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2307.00523.pdf)

## Quantum Neural Networks and Quantum Information
- [Quantum Neural Networks (QNNs)](https://patentimages.storage.googleapis.com/fa/61/94/ec20dc003a6517/US20200372334A1.pdf) (Patent Application)
- [The Role of Quantum Information in Thermodynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/1505.07835) (topical review)
